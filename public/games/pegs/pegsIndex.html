How to Play

Goal: Remove as many pegs as possible. A perfect game leaves one peg.

Select a peg, then select an empty hole exactly two spaces away. The peg must jump over one neighboring peg.

The jumped peg is removed. Diagonal and horizontal jumps are allowed wherever the board shape supports them.

Undo restores the previous board position, but it does not reduce your move count or rewind the timer.

Daily mode uses the same starting hole for everyone. Random mode is free play and does not save scores.
