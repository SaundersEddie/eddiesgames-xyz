        How to Play

        Goal: Slide the orange X block out through the EXIT on the right.

        Step 1: Click any block.

        Step 2: Glowing spaces show where that block can move.

        Step 3: Click a glowing space to slide the block there.

        Horizontal blocks only move left/right. Vertical blocks only move up/down.

        Your score is based on fewest moves, then fastest time.
