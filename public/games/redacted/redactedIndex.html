    How to Play

    Goal: Solve the case by finding the hidden secret word on the 5×5 grid.

    Trace a word by clicking adjacent letters (diagonals count). No tile reuse in a single word.

    Submit guesses that are 3–8 letters, are real words, and form a valid trace.

    You have 8 turns to crack the case.

    Intel: 🔥 Heat = shared letters (with duplicates).
    🎯 Order Hit = correct letters in the correct position.

    Daily Case uses a date-based seed. New Case generates a random puzzle.
