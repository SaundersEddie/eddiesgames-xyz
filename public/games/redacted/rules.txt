How to Play
Goal

Solve the case by finding the hidden secret word on the 5×5 letter grid.

Make a word

Click letters to trace a path through the grid.

Each new letter must be adjacent to the previous one (including diagonals).

You can’t reuse a tile in the same word.

Your current traced word appears at the top.

Submit a guess

Your guess must be 3–8 letters long.

It must be a real word (dictionary-backed).

It must be a valid trace on the grid.

You can’t submit the same word twice.

Hit Submit to lock in the guess, or Clear to start over.

Turns

You have 8 turns to find the secret word.

Find it in time → Case Closed

Run out of turns → Case Went Cold

Intel (your feedback)

After each valid guess you get two clues:

🔥 Heat = how many letters your guess shares with the secret word (counted with duplicates).

🎯 Order Hit = how many letters are correct and in the correct position (prefix match).

Use the intel to narrow down the secret word.

Daily vs New Case

Daily Case uses a date-based seed, so everyone gets the same puzzle that day.

New Case generates a fresh random puzzle.

Quick Tips

Start with medium-length words to gather better intel.

A high Heat with low Order Hit means you’ve got the right letters, wrong order.

A growing Order Hit usually means you’re locking onto the correct starting letters.
