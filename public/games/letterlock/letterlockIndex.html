How to Play

Goal: Open all six locks by building valid words from the letter bank.

Click letters to build a word. Each letter tile can be used once per word.

Submit real words of 3+ letters. Good words score points and may open one or more locks.

You get 10 bad guesses. Invalid words, too-short words, and blocked words cost one bad guess.

Daily gives everyone the same date-seeded puzzle. Free Play rolls a random one.

Daily Results: solved daily games are grouped by attempts, not ranked by names or time.
